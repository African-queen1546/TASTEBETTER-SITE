FAQs

Answers to common questions about Joshb's Depo and Tastebetter.

Everything you need to know about ordering, pickup, delivery, wholesale accounts, and returns — all in one place.

Ordering

Visit our Online Services page to browse available products, fill your cart, and submit your request. Alternatively, call us or come directly to the depot to place an order in person — our team will assist you on the spot.

Yes. Select "Pickup" at checkout and choose a convenient time slot. We'll prepare your items and send you a notification once the order is ready for collection at the depot. Bring your order confirmation when you arrive.

Same-day pickup is available for orders placed before 2:00 PM, subject to stock availability. For large or urgent orders, we strongly recommend calling ahead so we can confirm and prepare everything before you arrive.

The depot is open Monday to Saturday, 7:00 AM – 9:00 PM. We are closed on Sundays. Online orders can be submitted at any time and will be processed the next business morning.

Yes. You can place orders on behalf of a business or another person. Just include the correct delivery address or pickup name at checkout. For regular business orders, consider opening a wholesale account for better pricing and priority service.

Payment

We accept mobile money (MTN & Airtel), bank transfers, debit and credit cards, and cash at the depot. For business customers we can issue invoices — contact us to set this up before your first order.

Online and delivery orders require payment upfront to confirm the booking. Pickup customers can also pay on arrival at the depot. Wholesale account holders with approved credit terms may pay within the agreed period after delivery.

Changes and cancellations are accepted within one hour of placing an order, provided it has not yet been packed or dispatched. Contact us immediately through the Contact page or by phone. Refunds on cancelled orders are processed within 2–3 business days.

Online retail orders have a minimum of UGX 50,000. Wholesale orders typically start at UGX 200,000. We can discuss flexible arrangements for regular customers — reach out to discuss your needs.

Yes. A receipt is issued for every completed order — digitally by email or WhatsApp, and as a printed copy for depot pickups. Wholesale customers receive a formal invoice. Keep your receipt in case you need to reference or return an item.

Delivery

Yes. We deliver across Kampala and nearby areas. Delivery takes 1–3 business days depending on your location and order size. Fees are calculated at checkout based on distance and weight.

Once your order is dispatched, we send a notification by WhatsApp or email with a delivery update and estimated arrival time. If you need a real-time update, contact us directly on WhatsApp and we'll confirm your rider's status.

Our depot is in the main market district, easily accessible from major retail routes in Kampala. Visit the Contact page for the exact address, map pin, and directions.

Our rider will attempt to reach you by phone. If we cannot deliver, the order is held at the depot for up to 24 hours. You can arrange a redelivery or pick it up directly. A redelivery fee may apply for the second attempt.

We deliver Monday to Saturday. Deliveries are not available on Sundays. Orders placed on Saturday afternoons after 3:00 PM are scheduled for Monday morning unless you arrange a Saturday collection at the depot.

Products & Returns

All products display their manufacturing and expiry dates. We manage stock on a first-in, first-out basis and rotate items regularly. Any product close to expiry is removed from sale. If you have concerns about a specific item, our team will check it before packing your order.

We stock a wide range of fast-moving consumer goods (FMCG) including packaged foods, beverages, household essentials, and personal care products. Our range covers both everyday basics and branded items. Visit the Online Services page to browse the current product list.

Unopened items in their original packaging may be returned within 7 days of purchase with a valid receipt. Defective or damaged goods are replaced immediately once proof of purchase is provided. We do not accept returns on items that have been opened unless the product itself is faulty.

Yes, samples are available for selected products when stock allows. This is particularly useful before placing large wholesale orders. Contact our depot team to request samples and we'll arrange it ahead of your order.

Let us know what you need and we'll add it to the restock list and notify you when it arrives. For wholesale customers, we can often source specific products on request. Reach us via Contact or WhatsApp to check availability and lead times.

Wholesale & Business

Yes. Wholesale pricing is available for bulk purchases across most product categories. Prices vary by product and quantity. Share your order requirements and we'll provide a tailored quote — visit the Wholesale page to get started.

Register by contacting us through the Contact page or visiting the depot with your business details. We'll walk you through account setup, pricing tiers, and ordering terms. Most accounts are activated within one business day.

Discounts increase with order volume. Specific rates depend on the products and quantities involved. Request a quote for your exact mix and we'll show you the best pricing available for that order.

Credit terms are available for established wholesale customers with a consistent order history. Eligibility is assessed on a case-by-case basis. Contact us to discuss your situation and we'll advise on the steps required.

Yes. We supply retail shops, supermarkets, restaurants, schools, and other institutions across Kampala. If you have regular large-volume needs, we can set up a standing supply arrangement with scheduled deliveries. Reach out to discuss terms.
