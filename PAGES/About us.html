About us

Joshb's Depo powers Tastebetter for restaurants, chefs, and food entrepreneurs.

From depot pickup to online ordering, Tastebetter brings your kitchen smarter sourcing, faster logistics, and wholesale value in one place.

Our story

Joshb's Depo began as a small drinks shop and grew into a reliable depot for food businesses. Today, Tastebetter pairs depot convenience with digital ordering so customers can shop, collect, and move ahead with confidence.

Our mission

We give restaurants, caterers, and wholesale buyers the tools to source better, order smarter, and collect faster. Our focus is dependable supply, lower waste, and service built for growing food businesses.

Why choose us

Choose Tastebetter for depot convenience, seamless online ordering, and wholesale advantage. Every order is backed by stocked depot inventory, friendly support, and dependable pickup or delivery.
